📖 Introduction｜项目简介 
#### The Unmuted is a Web3-powered anonymous reporting and mutual aid system. By leveraging Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP) and decentralized storage, we dismantle the barriers of isolation and evidentiary challenges, creating a secure, transparent, and empathetic digital sanctuary for survivors.

”非默“是一款基于 Web3 技术的匿名举报与女性互助系统。我们利用零知识证明 (ZKP) 和去中心化存储技术，打破受害者在面对伤害时的孤立感与取证困境，构建一个安全、透明且充满温度的数字化避难所。

## ✨ Core Features 
### 🔐 3.1 Secure Evidence Vault

* **One-tap Capture:** Capture photo, video, and audio with instant AES-256 local encryption on the device.
* **Dual-Layer Proof:** Encrypted original evidence is stored on **Arweave**, while its unique fingerprint (Hash) is anchored on the blockchain.
* **Timestamp Anchoring:** Automatically generates immutable on-chain time certificates for every piece of evidence.

### 🛡️ 3.2 ZKP Community

* **Privacy Profiling:** Users verify identity (such as gender or residency) via **ZKP** without linking real-world social accounts.
* **Risk Alerts:** When multiple anonymous reports emerge in the same area, the system triggers a warning mechanism to alert nearby users.
* **P2P Support:** End-to-end encrypted anonymous chatrooms for survivors to support each other in a safe environment.

### ⚖️ 3.3 Support DAO

* **Legal/Mental Health Aid:** Survivors submit proposals, and the DAO disburses aid funds based on the validity of the evidence hash.
* **Professional Review:** Lawyers and mental health professionals join via **SBT (Soulbound Tokens)** to build a verified reputation system.
* **Collective Voting:** Fund allocation and major decisions are governed by the community to ensure the process is transparent and free from centralized manipulation.

## ✨ 核心功能

### 🔐 3.1 零知识匿名存证

* **一键快照：** 捕捉音视频，在设备本地即刻完成 AES-256 加密。
* **双重存证：** 加密原件存储于 **Arweave**，唯一指纹（Hash）存储于区块链。
* **时间戳锚定：** 自动为每一份证据生成不可伪造的链上时间证明。

### 🛡️ 3.2 匿名互助网

* **隐私画像：** 用户通过 ZKP 验证“女性”或“特定区域居民”身份，无需绑定真实社交账号。
* **风险预警：** 当同一区域出现多起匿名举报时，系统触发防扩散机制，向该区域用户发送预警提示。
* **点对点支持：** 建立端到端加密的匿名聊天室，让受害者在安全的环境下互助。

### ⚖️ 3.3 DAO 治理与救助

* **法律/心理援助：** 受害者发起提案，DAO 根据证据哈希的有效性拨付救助金。
* **专业评审：** 引入律师、心理咨询师持 **SBT（不可转让代币）** 入驻，建立信誉评估体系。
* **集体投票：** 资金流向、重大决策由社区共同治理，确保过程不受单一机构操控。
